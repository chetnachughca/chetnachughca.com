Who I am

"Curious about most things. Obsessed with very few."

I'm Chetna — a Chartered Accountant based in London. I love meeting people, learning from their experiences, and diving into things to understand them better. I'm someone who gets genuinely excited about ideas — whether that's a new AI tool, a tricky accounting problem, or a recipe I've never tried.

I built this website myself. I use Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini every day. I created my own GitHub account and figured out how to deploy a site — not because I'm a developer, but because I wanted to learn how it works. That's just how I'm wired.

Mathematics has been my first love since school — A* in every subject, Grade 9 across the board — but what really drives me is people. I love public speaking, long conversations, and learning from how others see the world. That curiosity is probably what earned me the nickname 'Miss Inquisitive' at university. I wear it proudly.
